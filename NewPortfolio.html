These are some examples of the work I have done while at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln.

Data Journalism


Private housing in downtown Lincoln

With the recent growth in private housing complexes in downtown Lincoln, I decided to look into how many rooms are being built and how they have affected university housing numbers. The article was published in The Daily Nebraskan.

Top 50 counties in the US

Using the USDA's Natural Amenities index, I found the top 50 counties in the United States to live in, and created a map that visually showed the index for each county.

Nebraska population change by county

While learning how to use QGIS, I used Nebraska population data to map the percent change by county.

Comparing university dining halls to downtown restaurants

To compare the sanitation level of the university dining halls to downtown restaurants, I requested health inspection reports for both the dining halls and restaurants.
